Welcome to Women In STEM


About Us

Our organization supports undergraduate women who have taken on the daunting task of perservering in majors or minors that rely primarily
on the sciences, technologies and mathematics - including engineering and health care professions.

There are four goals in the Women in STEM initiative:

1) Encourage female undergraduate students to stay in STEM majors (sciences,technology,engingeering,mathematics) and/or healthcare-related majors.

2) Provide female undergraduates with positive role models.

3) Create discussion on the issues facing women in STEM fields, both in the classroom and in the workplace.

4) Involve female undergraduates (and male allies) in the leadership and development of the #WomenInSTEM initiative.
